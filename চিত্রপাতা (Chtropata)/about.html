About চিত্রপাতা

চিত্রপাতা is a digital platform that allows photographers, studios, and clients to experience beautiful custom-designed photo albums on any device. Our mission is to blend traditional storytelling with modern technology by creating digital flip-through albums that feel just like printed ones—elegant, clean, and emotionally immersive.

Whether you're a wedding photographer, a travel blogger, or a family looking to preserve precious memories, চিত্রপাতা makes it easy to upload, organize, and view albums online in a format that replicates the feel of a real photo book.
