1. Primary Colors
Color Name	Hex Code	Usage Example	
Terracotta	#E07A5F	Buttons, Headers, Active Menu Items	
Cream White	#FFF1E6	Background, Cards, Section Backgrounds	
Olive Green	#81B29A	Button Hover, Accent Elements, Links	
Charcoal	#333333	Primary Text, Titles	
Warm Gray	#888888	Secondary Text, Subheadings, Captions

2. Typography
Headers & Titles:
Font Family: Playfair Display or Merriweather (serif fonts)
Color: Charcoal (#333333)
Weight: 700 (bold)

3. Buttons
Primary Button:
Background: Terracotta (#E07A5F)
Text: Cream White (#FFF1E6)
Border Radius: 6px
Box Shadow: subtle shadow for depth
Hover State: Olive Green (#81B29A) background, white text

4. Backgrounds
Main Background: Cream White (#FFF1E6) for a soft paper-like feel.

Sections: Alternate with subtle Terracotta (#E07A5F) overlays at 10-15% opacity for warmth.

Cards/Albums: White or very light cream with soft shadows to mimic physical album pages.

5. Navbar/Header
Background: Solid Terracotta (#E07A5F)

Text: Cream White (#FFF1E6)

Hover underline: Olive Green (#81B29A)

6. Image Frames / Album Cards
Border Radius: 8px

Box Shadow: soft shadow in Terracotta or Olive Green tints

Hover effect: slight scale-up with subtle shadow intensification

7. Additional Style
Page Flip Animation: For album viewing, subtle page-turn effect with a cream background and terracotta shadows.

Icons: Use Olive Green for active icons/buttons.

Links: Olive Green, underline on hover with terracotta highlight.
